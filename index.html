Meet Laura...

Hello, and welcome to the No Ordinary Gal podcast! I’m your host Laura and this is the show where we will be celebrating the normal, the average, the everyday gal and I’ll be sharing stories from ladies, who may seem to fit that bill, but to me are actually really rather extraordinary. So come along with us and discover how you too, are No Ordinary Gal!
